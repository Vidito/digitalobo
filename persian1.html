An Introduction to the Persian Language Pronouns and Colors


# Persian English
Zamir Pronoun
1 man I / me
2 to You (singular)
3 oo he / she (him / her)
4 maa we
5 shomaa You (plural / polite singular)
6 oonaa They (them)
verb ending
1 man ...... -am I am ....
2 to .......-i You are........
3 oo ...... -e He/she is ........
4 maa ........-im We are.........
4 shomaa ........-id/-in You are.........
5 oonaa ......-an They are ........

doctor => dok'tor

teacher => moal'lem

boy => pe'sar

girl => dokh'tar

lawyer => va'kil

engineer => mohan'des



Note

In Persian, unlike English, we don't use plural nouns after TO BE verbs in the following structure:
These are books: maa pesar-im OR They are girls: oonaa dokhtar-an

# Persian English

Colors

# Persian English
rang color
1 in che rang-e? What color is it?
2 inaa che rang-an? What color are these?
3 oonaa che rang-an? What color are those?
Answer
1 in sabz-e This is green
2 oon zard-e That is yellow
3 inaa aabi-an These are blue
4 oonaa siaah-an Those are black
5 in ketaab sefid-e This book is white
6 oon ketaab ghermez-e That book is red.
7 in ketaab'aa aabi-an These books are blue
7 oon ketaab'aa siaah-an Those books are black

Pragmatics Crash Course

  • by:VAHID NIAMADPOUR PhD RESEARCHER

You will love this free crash course if you are interested in these questions:

  • How do other people know what we mean by what we say if words have multiple meanings?
  • How do people get the sarcasm or irony if the meaning is different from the literal meaning?
  • How do we know that the sentence "the door is that way" MAY mean "get out!"?
  • or "Could you pass the salt?" is not a question but a request!
  • How do people READ BETWEEN THE LINES?
  • Why are politeness norms different from culture to culture?
And most importantly

What is Pragmatics?

To answer these questions and much much more, watch the videos. You will learn all about Context, Reference and Inference, Politeness Theory, Cooperative Principle, Gricean Maxims, Austin's Speech Act Theory, Face saving and Face Threatening Acts,...

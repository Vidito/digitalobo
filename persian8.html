the word BECAUSE

Because is Persian is 'chon' or 'chon ke'

Why is 'chera'
chera kaar mikoni? chon pool (money) mikhaam.
chera ghazaa mikhori? chon gorosne-am (hungry)
chera aab mikhoti? chon teshne-am (thirsty)
chera khoone miri? chon khaste-am (tired)
chera ketab mikhoni? chon doost daaram.
chera kabab nemikhori? chon vegetarian-am
chera kot mipooshi (wearing)? chon havaa sarde (cold)
chera farsi yaad migiri (learn)? because I want to.

Now, look at these words and complete the sentences :

yaad migiram = I learn
harf mizanam = I speak
esteraahat mikonam = I rest
khoone miram = I go home
mosaaferat miram = I go on a trip

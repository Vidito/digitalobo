# Persian Literal Translation English
1 in dar-e. this door is this is a door.
2 oon ketaab-e. that book is that is a book.
3 oo mard-e. he man is he is a man.
Negative
1 in dar nist. this door isn't this isn't a door.
2 oon ketaab nist. that book isn't that isn't a book.
Question
1 in dar-e? this door is? is this a door?
2 oon ketaab-e? that book is? is that a book?
Question and Answer
1 in dar-e? bale, in dare-e
2 oon ketaab-e? na, oon ketaab nist.
chi / WHAT
1 in chi-e? this what is? what is this?
2 oon chi-e? that what is? what is that?

An Introduction to the Present Tense in Persian

In the previous we learnt siple present tense. We continue with more complex verbs
Some verbs in Persian are made of two parts, that is they are a verb group, and include noun+dummy verb. The dummy verb is a word like "do".
Example: I work => man kaar mikonam=> I job(work) do .
I live in Holland=> man dar Holand zendegi mikonam==>I in Holland life do.


# Persian English
man komak mikonam I help
to goosh mikoni You listen
to negaah mikoni You look(watch)
1 oo kaar mikon-e S/He works
2 to doroogh mi-g-i You tell a lie (You lie)
2 man Holand ro doost daram I like Holland.

Man Vahid-am. Man moalemam va dar Holand zendegi mikonam. man baa Laura zendegi mikonam va oo espaniai-e. oo dar Amsterdam kaar mikone. Man Holand ro kheili doost daaram. injaa havaa kheili khoob nist vali mardom (people) kheili khooban.

Conversation 1

Minaa: salaam Sina. khoobi?
Sina: salaam Mina, khoobam, mamnoon. to chetori?
Mina: khoobam, mersi. emshab che kaar mikoni?
Sinaa: emshab miram khoone, shaam mikhoram, film mibinam, va mikhaabam. to chetor?
Mina: man emshab baa doostaam miram sinemaa. to baa maa miai?
Sina: na, nemitoonam. farda kheili kaar daram.
Mina: baashe, farda mibinamet.
Sina: ok, khodaafez.


Conversation 2

Minaa: salaam Sina. khoobi?
Sina: Minaa, raasti, to tanhaa zendegi mikoni?
Mina: na, ba pedar o maadaram zendegi mikonam. to chetor?
Sinaa: man tanhaa zendegi mikonam.
Mina: sakht nist?
Sina: na, kheili khoobe. raahat-am. baazi mikonam, film mibinam, aahang goosh mikonam. mehmooni miram.
Mina: che khoob! khosh be haalet!

Conversation 3

Minaa: salaam Sina. khoobi?
Sina: bad nistam, mersi. Mina, oon pesar ro mishnaasi?
Mina: aare, oon Braame. kheili baahooshe.
Sinaa: az koja midooni?
Mina: oon se taa zaboon balade.
Sinaa: baa ham dars mikhoonid?
Mina: na, oon Mohandesi mikhoone. man fizik mikhoonam. to chand taa zaboon baladi?
Sina: man ham se taa zaboon baladam vali khengam.
Mina: to khengi, vali oon baahooshe.

Adding ‘i’ to nouns
when asking questions like “what books do you like?” what food do you eat”,… we normally add ‘I’ to the words FOOD or BOOk,…
What book= che ketaabi? --- what food= che ghazaa-i?
What book do you want? Che ketaab-i ro mikhai?
What food do you like? Che ghazaa-i ro doost daari?
We can also use the same sentences with a question word like WHICH, in that case you don’t need ‘I’:
Which book do you want? = Kodoom ketaab ro mikhai?
Kodoom ghazaa ro doost daari?
Using the following words, make question: shahr= city / keshvar= country
What country do you like? What city do you like?
which country do you like? Which city do you like?

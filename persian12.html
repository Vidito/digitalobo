Grammar

Possessive particles

Look at these examples:
ketaab-am = my book (you may read it as "i am a book") :D
ketaab-et = Your book
ketaab-esh = his/her book
ketaab-emoon = our book
ketaab-etoon = your (plural) book
ketaab-eshoon = their book
Examples:
ketaab-am kojaast? (or kojaa-e)
khoone-et kojaast? where is your house?
maashin-esh chi-e? what is his car? (what make is it?)

Past Tense (was / were)

I was = bood-am.
You were = bood-i
He/she was = bood
we were = bood-im
you were = bood-id / bood-in
they were = bood-an

Examples: man dishab (last night) khoone bood-am
to dirooz (yesterday) park boodi

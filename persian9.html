Salaam. Man Vahid hastam va si o shish saal daaram. Man mikhaam darbaareye zendegi dar Holand harf bezanam. Zendegi dar Holand kheyli khoobe. Mardom kheyli khoob-an vali havaa khoob nist.
Zendegi inja raahat-e (comfortable) vali baayad kheyli kaar bekoni. Man nemitoonam kheyli kaar bekonam chon ke dars mikhoonam vali pool ham nadaaram. Khaak too saram! :D
Man har rooz (every day) che kaar mikonam?
Man sobh (morning) saate 5 bidar misham (wake up), va sobhoone (breakfast) mikhoram. Saate 8 ketaab mikhoonam taa (until) saate 11. Saate 11 naahaar mikhoram va esteraahat mikonam (rest). Ba’d (after) az naahaar, ghahve mikhoram va ketaab mikhoonam. Saate 4 miram biroon (out). Saate 6 bar migardam (come back) khoone va ghazaa mipazam (cook). Saate 8 shaam mikhoram. Ba’d film mibinam va mikhaabam.
To har rooz tooye khoone che kaar mikoni?
